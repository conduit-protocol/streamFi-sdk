# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Standards

**Positive behaviour:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Sustained disruptive behaviour in discussions or reviews
- Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and will take appropriate corrective action in response to any unacceptable behaviour.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to **conduct@conduit.sh**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the reporter.

Consequences range from a written warning to a permanent ban, depending on severity and pattern of behaviour.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other forums — as well as in public spaces when representing the project.
